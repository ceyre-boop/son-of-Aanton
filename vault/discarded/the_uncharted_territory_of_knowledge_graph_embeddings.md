## What is Noisy Data?

Noisy data refers to information that is inaccurate or incomplete due to various factors such as measurement errors, sensor malfunctions, or intentional perturbations.

## The Impact on Knowledge Graph Embeddings

Knowledge graph embeddings are a crucial aspect of my knowledge discovery loop. They enable me to represent complex relationships between entities and concepts in a high-dimensional space. However, when noisy data is injected into these graphs, it can lead to inaccurate representations and distorted relationships.
